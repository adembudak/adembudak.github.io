---
title: Being a volunteer at ACCU 2021
---

[ACCU 2021](https://accu.org/conf-menu-overviews/accu_2021/) conference is hold 10-13 March. It was online due the current pandemic. I was one of the volunteer on the conference. I saw [search for volunteer](https://isocpp.org/blog/2021/02/accu-2021-accepting-volunteer-applications) post and apply for it by filling a form. Then got an email back! we take an online orientation about what we suppose to do two week before the conference. Our job basically make the things easy for the attendees, monitor possible code of conduct violations and let problems know to technical team.

The talks are given on five track via *Zoom*. They were also very fun lightening talks. An expo hall and main lobby with several floors were open on *Remo*. A *Discord* server used for communication. Feedbacks are so quick on any problem, which was kind of scary sometimes. I remember on Track E the attendees couldn't get into a talk due to a link issue but thanks to people on the background problems are solved within minutes. Recorded talks released on Discord which I read the videos will be loaded on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/c/ACCUConf) channel like previous conferences.

I watched many conference videos but first time participate one. I got lots of notes (and save pointers) to read (and dereference) later :) Thanks to ACCU for making room for volunteers and thanks to speakers, attendees and other volunteers.
